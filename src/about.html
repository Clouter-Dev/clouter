About Us
Clouter is a centralized platform built to streamline communication between college clubs and students.
Clubs can manage and share event schedules, announcements, and updates, while students can easily
track and stay informed about upcoming events.The platform enhances campus engagement
by making event discovery and reminders simple and efficient.

    Features
  • Centralized event calendar
  • Club-wise event listings
  • Real-time notifications
  • Mobile-responsive design
